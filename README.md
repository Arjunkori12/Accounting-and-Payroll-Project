# Accounting-and-Payroll-Project
Complete Excel-based accounting system: 30-employee payroll, AR aging (50+ invoices), bank &amp; credit card reconciliation, month-end adjustments, and Canadian tax compliance (HST/WSIB/EHT). Demonstrates advanced Excel and full accounting cycle proficiency.

### Background
This accounting project was created to bridge the gap between theoretical accounting knowledge and practical, real-world application. Many accounting students and professionals struggle to find comprehensive, realistic datasets to practice their skills. This project solves that problem by providing a **complete, end-to-end accounting system** for a fictional vehicle dealership.

### What Makes This Project Unique

**Real Business Complexity:**
Unlike simplified textbook examples, this project includes the messy reality of business accounting:
- Employees with different pay structures (hourly vs. salaried)
- Customers with varying payment histories and aging balances
- Bank errors that need investigation and correction
- Missing documentation requiring follow-up
- Trade-in transactions with outstanding liens
- Multi-step journal entries for complex transactions

**Canadian Regulatory Compliance:**
Accurately reflects Canadian business requirements:
- HST (Harmonized Sales Tax) calculations and filing
- CPP (Canada Pension Plan) and EI (Employment Insurance) deductions
- WSIB (Workplace Safety Insurance Board) premiums
- EHT (Employer Health Tax) for Ontario employers
- Proper tax remittance and reconciliation procedures

**Industry-Specific Scenarios:**
Automotive dealership operations including:
- New and used vehicle sales accounting
- Trade-in processing with payoff handling
- Add-on product revenue (warranties, protection packages)
- Financing arrangements and commission tracking
- Inventory management and cost of goods sold

**Professional Excel Implementation:**
- Named tables for easy formula referencing
- VLOOKUP and INDEX-MATCH for data relationships
- Dynamic calculations using TODAY() and date functions
- Automated journal entry generation with SUMIF formulas
- Pivot tables for management reporting
- Conditional formatting for visual alerts
- Data validation for input controls

### Business Context

**Company:** Premier Auto Group (fictional)
**Industry:** Automotive Sales & Service
**Location:** Ontario, Canada
**Size:** 30 employees across sales, service, parts, finance, and management
**Annual Revenue:** ~$2.2M
**Fiscal Year:** Calendar year

**Departments:**
- Sales (9 employees) - New and used vehicle sales
- Service (7 employees) - Vehicle maintenance and repairs
- Parts (5 employees) - Automotive parts inventory
- Finance (4 employees) - Accounting and administration
- Management (5 employees) - Leadership and oversight

### Project Scope

This project covers a **one-month accounting period (December 2024)** and includes:

1. **Weekly payroll processing** for all 30 employees
2. **Accounts receivable management** with 50 customer invoices
3. **Daily bank reconciliation** with outstanding items and errors
4. **Monthly credit card reconciliation** with expense categorization
5. **Month-end adjusting entries** (6+ entries)
6. **Quarterly tax filing** (HST return for Q4 2024)
7. **Annual tax calculations** (WSIB and EHT reconciliation)
8. **Complex sales transactions** (new and used vehicles)

### Why Excel?

While many businesses use specialized accounting software (QuickBooks, Sage, SAP), Excel remains the most versatile and widely-used tool for:
- Financial analysis and modeling
- Custom reporting and dashboards
- Data import/export and transformation
- Ad-hoc calculations and reconciliations
- Budget planning and forecasting

This project teaches **transferable skills** that apply to any accounting system, while demonstrating advanced Excel proficiency that employers value.
